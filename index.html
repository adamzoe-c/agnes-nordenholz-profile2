A conversation with Anna Wendt, film producer

When I first met Anna, we immediately connected through our shared concern for our mother, who needed care. At that time, we were both intensely focused on organizing her care, confronted with dementia in all its facets, bureaucracy with health insurance companies and authorities, absurd everyday situations, and emotional exhaustion.

We talked about responsibility and grief, guilt, and the struggle not only for our mothers but also for our own livelihoods.

The philosophy of Agnes Nordenholz

AGNES NORDENHOLZ is a slow luxury label founded in Berlin in 2015. Agnes Schorer is the creative mind behind the brand. Our goal is to create products with "soul" that can withstand the fast-paced society of our time.

By choosing traditional craftsmanship and high-quality materials processed in Europe according to ethical guidelines, we ensure every product holds lasting value.
