Pants and shorts for women

Unravel the essence of Berlin’s tailoring with our pants for women, meticulously crafted in Germany. And most important: we offer pants for women with working pockets! Why I mention the pockets? It’s a regular thing for male customers, but the fashion industry often adds no pockets or not working pockets/ fake pockets to women’s wear, so it’s not adding volume.

The Importance of Pockets

Which is obviously nonsense, because women need pockets as much as men! Our designs focus on functionality without compromising the silhouette. Experience the perfect blend of practical detail and high-end tailoring in every piece of our collection.
